PawPad Privacy Policy

Effective June 6, 2026

PawPad is a pet food and health diary. This policy explains what data the app handles and where it lives. The short version: your data stays in your own iCloud account — we never see it.

What PawPad stores

  • Pet details you enter: name, species, and an optional photo (stored as a small thumbnail).
  • Feeding entries: timestamps, food type and amounts, water amounts.
  • Caretaker names, roles, and avatar choices for people in your household.
  • A change history of entries (who added or edited what, and when), so your household can see an entry's story.

Where your data lives

All data is stored on your device and in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit service. PawPad has no servers of its own. The developer cannot access, read, or recover your data — it is protected by your Apple ID under Apple's privacy terms.

Household sharing

If you invite caretakers, the pet's diary is shared with the people you choose through Apple's iCloud sharing. Invited caretakers can see the shared pet's entries and history. You can stop sharing at any time from the app or from your iCloud settings.

What PawPad does not do

  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising of any kind.
  • No third-party SDKs or data processors.
  • No accounts, sign-ups, or passwords — just your existing Apple ID via iCloud.
  • Your data is never sold, shared, or transmitted to anyone other than Apple's iCloud on your behalf.

Children

Kid caretaker profiles are just a name and an avatar chosen by a parent — they have no Apple ID, no account, and no data is collected from or about children by the developer.

Deleting your data

Deleting a pet in the app deletes its diary from your iCloud. You can also manage iCloud data for PawPad in iOS Settings → your Apple ID → iCloud. Deleting the app from your device does not by itself delete data already stored in your iCloud.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this page with a new effective date.

Contact
