DillyPad Privacy Policy

Effective June 11, 2026

DillyPad is a pet food and health diary. This policy explains what data the app handles and where it lives.

What DillyPad stores

  • Your display name, as shared by Apple when you first sign in with Sign in with Apple.
  • Pet details you enter: name, species, and an optional photo (stored as a small thumbnail).
  • Feeding entries: timestamps, food type and amounts, water amounts.
  • Caretaker roles for people in your household who share a pet with you.
  • A change history of entries (who added or edited what, and when).

Where your data lives

Data is stored on DillyPad's servers, secured by your Apple ID. You sign in with Sign in with Apple — DillyPad never receives or stores your Apple ID password. You can revoke DillyPad's access at any time in iOS Settings → your Apple ID → Password & Security → Apps Using Apple ID.

Household sharing

If you invite caretakers, the shared pet's diary is visible to the people you choose. Caretakers can see and add entries for shared pets. You can remove caretakers from within the app at any time.

What DillyPad does not do

  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising of any kind.
  • No third-party SDKs or data processors.
  • Your data is never sold or shared with any third party.

Deleting your data

You can delete individual pets and entries from within the app. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us via the support page below.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this page with a new effective date.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Reach out via the DillyPad support page.
